Arepa's Recipe

  1. Put the Harina Pan (corn flour) in a bowl
  2. Put cheese in it in the bowl
  3. Put a bit of butter
  4. Put slowly water
  5. Mix everything
  6. Make small arepas (disk, flat breads)
  7. Put on a hot pan and brown on each side
  8. Enjoy
